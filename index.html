Learn a language.

An obvious one but what better way to start thinking about all the holidays you’ll be able to take once this is over than by learning the language of the countries you visit.Plus, it’s always useful to have on your CV and if working abroad has been on your mind, then get a head start by picking up the local lingo.

Learn photography.

Avid photography fans or those new to the craft can pick up tips, polish existing skills and further your interest with an online photography class.

Learn Dancing.

Up your dance game with at-home classes, you can follow along with online, going at your own pace and being right next to a shower when you’ve worked up a sweat.

Learn to code.

Learning to code can make you more self-sufficient,teach you how to think and take your career to new heights. It is a skill that every one should possess in the new world!
